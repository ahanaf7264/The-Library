The Library

Welcome to the world of books

Our library is a quiet and inspiring place designed for learning, research, and creativity. It holds a wide collection of books covering general knowledge, science, and technology, with a special focus on Electrical and Electronic Engineering (EEE). Students can explore topics such as circuit analysis, power systems, electronics, and modern engineering innovations. Alongside technical resources, the library also offers science journals and reference materials that support academic growth and research work. With a calm environment and well-organized sections, the library encourages deep focus, critical thinking, and continuous learning for students, researchers, and anyone passionate about science and engineering.
