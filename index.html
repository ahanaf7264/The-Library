Our library is a quiet and inspiring place designed for learning, research, and creativity. It holds a wide collection of books covering general knowledge, science, and technology, with a special focus on Electrical and Electronic Engineering (EEE). Students can explore topics such as circuit analysis, power systems, electronics, and modern engineering innovations. Alongside technical resources, the library also offers science journals and reference materials that support academic growth and research work. With a calm environment and well-organized sections, the library encourages deep focus, critical thinking, and continuous learning for students, researchers, and anyone passionate about science and engineering.

Engineering Library


This library is specially designed for engineering students, especially those from the EEE department. Here, students can find a wide collection of electrical, electronics, and engineering-related books. The library includes textbooks, reference books, journals, and project guides that help students understand both theoretical concepts and practical applications. A quiet and well-organized environment makes it easier for students to study, research, and improve their technical knowledge. This library plays an important role in building a strong foundation for future engineers and supporting them in their academic journey.

Looking to own the books you love? Our library offers a curated selection of textbooks, reference materials, and engineering guides available for purchase. Whether you’re preparing for exams or expanding your knowledge, find the right books at student-friendly prices—right here in our library.
